# 8. Conclusion

NexaCity proves that by combining low-cost ESP32 hardware with a modern WebSocket architecture, we can successfully replace rigid legacy logic with a reactive, zero-latency smart zone network.

### **Total Impact**
- **Smarter Cities:** Democratizes urban telemetry, allowing municipalities to safely upgrade their infrastructure without expensive, proprietary vendor lock-in.
- **Traffic Reduction:** Our autonomous threshold algorithms detect early bottlenecks, instantly triggering manual interventions to measurably reduce cascading gridlock.

### **Future Work**
- **Deep AI Integration:** Implementing predictive Machine Learning models into our existing Node.js services to anticipate traffic density before it occurs.
- **Multi-Zone Scaling:** Expanding our decoupled Docker architecture from a single local crosswalk simulation into a massive, fully synchronized digital grid.
